Our Story

Akshara Event Planners began in 2015 with a single dream: to turn ordinary moments into unforgettable celebrations. Since then we've crafted over 350+ events, learning on the go, overcoming challenges and growing with every celebration. Our team brings warmth, attention to detail and a personal touch to every wedding, party and corporate event.

In our early days we faced long nights, tight budgets and venues that tested our patience — but each challenge made us better. We learned to design beautiful setups on small budgets, build trusted vendor relationships, and deliver calm leadership on hectic days. Today, Akshara is more than a service — it's a promise to craft joyful, elegant experiences that families remember for life.

Every event we do is guided by the same values: empathy, creativity and reliability. Whether it's a traditional wedding, an intimate ceremony, or a large corporate launch, our goal is the same — flawless execution so you can be present and enjoy each moment. and We are the best event planners in Tirupati.
